Welcome to HandyCrew4You

Reliable handyman work, tree trimming, and exterior cleaning — done right every time.

Serving Northwest Crossing, Old Farm District, Boyd Acres, Midtown, and beyond in Bend, Oregon.

HandyCrew4You is a local service focused on practical fixes, outdoor upkeep, and helping folks around Bend keep things in shape. It's not always fancy — and some of the work might even look like you did it yourself (in the best way). But it’s clean, reliable, and done with care.

Services Offered

General Maintenance

  • Weed whacking and yard upkeep
  • Gutter clearing and minor exterior cleanup
  • Recurring monthly or seasonal service packages

Pressure Washing

  • Driveways, patios, sidewalks
  • Vinyl siding, decks, fences
  • Moss and mildew removal

Tree & Brush Work

  • Overhanging limb and borken branch removal
  • Small tree and brush clearing
  • Chainsaw work & debris haul-off

Note: We do not offer high-climb or large-scale removals.

Handyman

  • Light carpentry & patching
  • Furniture assembly, appliance installs
  • Basic plumbing & fixture swaps

Frequently Asked Questions

🧹 Do you offer general maintenance contracts?

Yes — we offer flexible maintenance contracts for recurring needs like weed whacking, gutter cleaning, pressure washing, seasonal cleanups, and more.

💲 How much do you charge?

Rates vary by service type and scope. Most clients can expect:

Service Type On-Demand Rate Notes
Exterior Maintenance Bid Work Recurring packages available
Tree & Brush Work $100/hour Site visit may be required
Pressure Washing $150-$250 Based on surface & square footage
Handyman Services $100/hour 1-hour minimum + materials
🔁 Do you offer ongoing services?

Yes — We're all about the callback - maintenance packages and seasonal work can be tailored to your schedule.

📍 What areas do you service?

We serve Bend, Oregon and surrounding areas — mostly the Old Farm District. Generaly more remote building or estate maintenance available upon request.

🧭 What differentiates HandyCrew4You from others?

While most services stay close to town, HandyCrew4You can offer services in remote or scenic locations — farms, ranches, and properties off the beaten path. Whether it's a ranch out in Tumalo, a property with a view, or a remote job that requires a little extra adventure — count me in. Bonus points if there’s horseback riding or an ATV involved (seriously). I’m selective about the jobs I take out of town, but I genuinely enjoy meaningful, honest work in unique settings.

🛡️ Are you licensed and insured?

HandyCrew4You is not currently insured. We only take on projects within our skillset, and all work is agreed upon upfront with waivers signed if necessary.
