Rates & Scheduling

All pricing is based on the specific scope of work — no one-size-fits-all rates. Whether it’s trimming branches, clearing brush, or cutting down and hauling away a tree, each job is evaluated individually based on size, access, safety, and cleanup needs.

  • 🌿 Brush Clearing & Light Trimming: Priced by area and density — send photos for a quick estimate.
  • 🪓 Limb & Small Tree Removal: Rates vary depending on height, thickness, and structure — great for dead or overhanging limbs, or trees under ~20 feet.
  • 🚛 Hauling & Cleanup: Available for most jobs — pricing depends on volume and loads. I use a large trailer for efficient removal.

Availability: Evenings and weekends; weekday slots available as needed. Reach out with your project details to check availability and get a quote.
