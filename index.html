Frequently Asked Questions

🧹 Do you offer general maintenance contracts?

Yes — we offer flexible maintenance contracts for recurring needs like weed whacking, gutter cleaning, pressure washing, seasonal cleanups, and more.

💲 How much do you charge?

Rates vary by service type and scope. Most clients can expect:

  • Exterior Maintenance – $50/hour
  • Tree & Brush Work – $75/hour (site visit required for bid)
  • Pressure Washing – Flat rate by surface type
  • Handyman Services – $100/hour (1 hr min + materials)
🔁 Do you offer ongoing services?

Yes — long-term client relationships are welcome. Maintenance packages and seasonal work can be tailored to your schedule.

📍 What areas do you service?

We serve Bend, Oregon — including Northwest Crossing, Midtown, Boyd Acres, and surrounding neighborhoods.

🛡️ Are you licensed and insured?

HandyCrew4You is not currently insured. We only take on projects within our skillset, and all work is agreed upon upfront with waivers signed if necessary.
